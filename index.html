What is DarkNodeAI?

DarkNodeAI is a modern stack for bringing LLMs into ethical hacking and defensive security. It runs on commodity hardware (even 16 GB RAM) using 4‑bit/8‑bit quantization, exposes a clean REST API, and pairs with a static frontend (GitHub Pages) or your own React/Next.js app.
